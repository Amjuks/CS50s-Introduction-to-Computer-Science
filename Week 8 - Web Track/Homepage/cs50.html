My CS50 Story

It was around June that I had joined this course. Everything
I knew until then was just Scratch and common C syntax.
I had my school so I had to manage my timefor this course.
The first 2 weeks in this course helped me a bit to
understand what programming was all about. After 2 months
I was in week 5 and before I knew I had learnt C.

My first program was to print Hello World! and in the
next 2 months I had learnt to filter images, spell check a
text file and much more! The next day I had already learnt python
and use it in the same way I was using C.

I was off for sometime after that since I had school. When I came
back I had learnt SQL in a week and learnt to manage databases
using it, which was pretty fun! The next day I watched a few
lectures in the web track and here I am now!

I have made a few programs here that do the same, have fun!
